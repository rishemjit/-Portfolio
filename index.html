About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Rishemjit Kaur who is working as a Senior Principal Scientist with CSIR-Central Scientific Instruments Organisation Chandigarh. I'm passionate about analysing and interpreting large amount of data from a range of sources using algorithmic, data mining, artificial intelligence, machine learning and statistical tools, in order to make it accessible to food processing instrumentation lab.

I love to build algorithms and design experiments to merge, manage, interrogate and extract data to provide better results for the data visualization. I love to design data modeling processes to create algorithms and predictive models.

I look for opportunities to use insights/datasets/code/models across other research organisation.
